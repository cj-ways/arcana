# Arcana Research Standards

## Research Before Acting
Before generating recommendations, search the web for current best practices, competitor approaches, and known solutions. Ground every suggestion in evidence — codebase findings or external research with sources.

## Evidence-Based Decisions
Every recommendation must cite specific evidence: a line of code, a competitor feature, a documentation reference, or a verified web source. If you cannot cite evidence, do not recommend.

## Use Available Resources
When analysis requires deep investigation, use parallel subagents to explore multiple angles simultaneously. Use WebSearch for external context. Use Explore agents for codebase analysis. Do not leave capabilities unused when they would improve quality.
