# Arcana Methodology Standards

## Multi-Perspective Analysis
Examine features and code from multiple relevant perspectives. Determine which perspectives apply dynamically based on the feature type — do not use a fixed checklist for every situation.

## Dynamic Over Static
Adapt analysis to the specific context. Hardcoded checklists miss nuance. Discover what matters for THIS specific feature, project, and domain through research and codebase analysis.

## Plan Thoroughly, Execute Fast
Take time to analyze, research, and plan before implementing. Once the plan is confirmed, execute efficiently without second-guessing. Thoroughness in planning prevents bugs in execution.

## Persist Knowledge for Continuity
When discoveries are made that would benefit future sessions or other agents, persist them to the appropriate location (CLAUDE.md, MEMORY.md, docs/). Every session should leave the project better documented than it found it.
